" Fill Your Life With Experiences, Not Things. Have Stories to Tell, Not Stuff to Show"

I want to share one of my valueble experience that i gained last year, in 2020. When the entire country affected by Covid-19

Pandemic crisis, movement control order, MCO had been restricted to all citizens and thus, this situation had affected

my family source of income. To overcome this, my sisters and I planned to start a small business to find side income.

We decided to sell korean food, which is Gimbap and also churros. We learn all the recipes and steps to make Gimbap and churros

through youtube and social media. The ingredients and tools are affordable and easy to purchase. We start selling our product

through Facebook and WhatsApp application. We are selling Gimbap and churros through online and do the cash on delivery (COD) service.



Doing food business taught me to be responsible, manage my time wisely, and strong. This is because, I need to be

consistent, determined and not easily give up when doing business. At the begining, I only join food delivery groups

at Facebook and food delivery business groups at Telegram app. I am doing all the process from the photoshoot, cost

and price calculation together with my sisters. My sisters and I will wake up as early as 4 to 5 a.m to prepare the

ingredients if we got a lot of orders. To finish all the orders every day, it will took around 3 to 4 hours at the

kicthen. During the mid semester break, we manage to receive and make orders every day including weekend.I am so happy

when the customers give positive feedbacks after they tested the Gimbap and churros. When new semester begin, it is very

struggle for me and my sisters to do the orders, Gimbap and churros because three of us had online classess everyday in

the morning until afternoon. Sometimes, we need to make Gimbap while listening to our lecture lecturing through Google Meet,

and go to delivery the orders right after classes end. Thus, we nned to be strong and manage our time wisely, to study and

doing business at the same time. Thus, this experience had taught me valuable lesson and memorable experiences.
